Other Work

Overtures (Thesis Project)

In 1977, NASA attached two phonographic records to Voyager 1 and 2. Each record contains images and audio that were carefully selected in order to portray the diversity of Life on Earth. As my thesis project, Overtures was a virtual gallery that acts as an extension to these records.

Arboretum Explorer

The ArbExplorer is an interactive map that lets you explore the Arnold Arboretum in Boston. As part of our website redesign for the Arboretum, I was tasked with reimaging what the ArbExplorer's full potential could be.

Communication Catalyst

Communication Catalyst is a narrative website and set of newspapers that explore the effects of Hurricane Katrina on the communication and structural systems within New Orleans.

NASA Photo Archives

The existence of NASA is rooted in the combination of humanities incessant need to both learn and explore. This project is a redesign on their photo gallery to relfect these sentiments.

The Prince of Cool

Chet Baker was a jazz musician in the mid 1900’s. He was known for his soft and melodic trumpet melodies, with wispy and vulnerable vocal melodies laid on top. This box set commemorates his legacy.
